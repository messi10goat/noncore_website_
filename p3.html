Can you give a general discription of the interview with some interview questions?

Both the rounds were PI and Discussion. Mostly Questions were based on my previous work experience and projects (How I did, what challenges I faced and what user impact I had). Other few questions were Guestimates and Situations based.


How did you prepare for it?

For the Strategy Roles firms generally prefer the MBAs but if you have some previous experience of building the products from the scratch and taking it to market, I think one can be considered. There is no particular prep material for this but yes practicing the Guestimates, situation based questions (somewhat similar to case) and having a good knowledge of different frameworks for defining a business problem and solution can surely help.


Any tips for juniors?

1) Either you have good Grades or a Killing profile
2) Focus more on real-time projects (build products,work with startups) rather than reading and taking courses if you are interested in Non-Core with a Tech Degree.
3) Read daily atleast the Business Insider,Entrepreneur,TechCrunch,Inc42 and Economics Times 4)Build your network over Linkedin
