How did you get to know about opportunity?

On Campus


What is your Job Profile?

Data Analyst


Can you give a general discription of the interview with some interview questions?

1. Written Round: Basic Aptitude Questions
2. Group Discussion (Skype; 10-15 mins): A general/ current affairs topic was given to a group of ~9 students. 2-3 were selected from each group.
3. Personal Interview (Skype; 20-30 mins)


How did you prepare for it?

"Aptitude Test: CAT preparation
GD: 2-3 rounds of practice GD with friends
PI: Revision of projects on my Resume, Practiced puzzles of Geeksforgeeks, Revised basics of ML, Deep Learning"


Any tips for juniors?

1. Be enthusiastic and inquisitive. 2. Revise each and every Skill, Project, POR, Extracurricular and Course on your Resume. DON'T LIE IN YOUR CV 3. Change your resume according to the job profile 4. Practice puzzles, guesstimates and case studies especially for Non-core roles 5. Prepare your "Intro" beforehand, work on your soft-skills
